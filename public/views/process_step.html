Process steps for building your TrueSelf Leadership profile:



    Step 1: Reflective Questions 30 min
    Authentically fill 3 reflective questions and an online questionnaire.


    Step 2: Assigning TrueSelf Leadership Template 1 hour
    Facilitator interacts with you to assign your unique Leadership profile template.


    Step 3: Building TrueSelf Leadership Profile 1.5 hours
    Build your online Leadership Profile using the assigned template.
